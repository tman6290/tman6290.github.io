About the Artist

Hi, I'm Tofarati
aka Art_By_Tofa

I'm a self-taught artist with a deep passion for anime-style illustration and sequential storytelling. Every character I draw carries a personality — from their silhouette down to the smallest design detail.

My primary tools are Clip Studio Paint for comics and illustrations, and various design software for graphic work. I'm always pushing to grow — one page at a time.
